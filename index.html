02 · Flight Sequence

How I Work

Every mission I touch follows the same loop — and each pass through it makes the next prediction better.

T−02

Model the mission

High-fidelity propagation with full force models — gravity harmonics, drag, SRP, third-body — plus coverage and access analysis, link budgets, and delta-V trades before anything flies.

T−01

Automate the pipeline

Turn one-off analyses into Python, Rust, and MATLAB tools the whole team can rerun — orbit determination, maneuver planning, reporting, and QC without the babysitting.

T+00

Close the loop on orbit

Run orbit determination on every pass, compare predicted vs. actual state after every maneuver, and feed the residuals back into the force models and thrust calibration.
